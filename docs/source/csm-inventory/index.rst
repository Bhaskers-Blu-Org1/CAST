CSM Inventory
=============

CSM can store information about your cluster. One category of this information is the physical hardware inventory and statistics of your cluster. Once collected this data is stored in the `CSM Database`_. 

.. _CSM Database: https://cast.readthedocs.io/en/latest/csmdb/index.html
